Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI deal ingestion work? expand_more

Simply upload a PDF document (CIM, teaser, or NDA) and our AI will automatically extract key information including company name, financials, deal size, industry, and more. The entire process takes just seconds.

Is my data secure? expand_more

Yes. We use AES-256 encryption for data at rest and TLS 1.3 for data in transit. We are SOC 2 Type II compliant and conduct regular security audits. Your deal data is never used to train AI models.

Can I integrate PE OS with my existing tools? expand_more

Yes! PE OS offers a comprehensive REST API and integrations with popular tools like Outlook, Gmail, and leading data providers. Enterprise plans include custom integration support.

What's included in the free trial? expand_more

The 14-day free trial includes full access to all Mid-Market tier features. No credit card required. You can import deals, use AI features, and invite team members during the trial period.
