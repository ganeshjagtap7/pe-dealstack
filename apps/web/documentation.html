Quick Start Guide

1

Create Your Account

Visit the sign up page and enter your email, full name, and firm name. You'll receive a verification email to confirm your account.

Once verified, log in and you'll be taken to your dashboard. The account creator is automatically assigned the Admin role.

2

Import Your First Deal

Navigate to Deal Intake from the sidebar. Upload a CIM, teaser, or any deal document (PDF, Word, Excel).

Our AI extracts key data — company name, financials, industry — in seconds. Review the extraction preview with confidence scores before saving.

3

Invite Your Team

Go to Settings and invite colleagues via email. Assign roles: Admin (full control), Member (standard access), or Viewer (read-only).

Invitees receive an email link to join your workspace. All team members share the same deal pipeline and data room.

Feature Guides

upload_file

Deal Ingestion

Upload documents, paste text, scrape URLs, or forward emails to create deals

expand_more

File Upload

Upload PDF, Word (.docx), Excel (.xlsx, .csv), or plain text files. The system automatically detects the file type and routes it to the appropriate parser. Excel files with multiple deals are processed as bulk imports.

Text Paste

Paste deal content directly — from emails, Slack messages, WhatsApp forwards, or meeting notes. Minimum 50 characters required. Select the source type for better AI extraction context.

URL Research

Enter a company website URL and PE OS scrapes multiple pages (About, Team, Products, Services) to build a comprehensive company profile. Optionally preview the extraction before creating a deal.

Email Forwarding

Upload .eml email files exported from Gmail or Outlook. PE OS extracts deal data from the email body and automatically processes any PDF attachments.

Confidence Scores

Every extracted field gets a confidence score (0-100%). Fields below 60% are automatically flagged for manual review. Color-coded bars show extraction quality at a glance: green (>80%), yellow (60-80%), red (<60%).

Deal Management

Track deals through your pipeline from initial review to close

expand_more

Deal Pipeline

View all deals in your CRM with filtering by stage (Initial Review, Due Diligence, LOI, Closing, Closed, Passed), status (Active, On Hold, Dead), and industry. Sort by date, name, or deal size.

Deal Details

Each deal page shows company info, financials, AI-extracted data, activity timeline, attached documents, team members, and investment memos. Edit any field directly on the deal page.

Deal Team

Assign team members to specific deals with access levels: View (read-only), Edit (modify deal data), or Admin (full control including delete). Track who's working on what.

Activity Feed

Every action on a deal is logged — document uploads, stage changes, AI extractions, memo creation, team changes. The timeline gives a complete audit trail of deal progress.

psychology

AI Features

AI-powered extraction, chat, memo generation, and multi-document analysis

expand_more

AI Data Extraction

Powered by GPT-4, the system extracts company name, industry, revenue, EBITDA, margins, employee count, headquarters, and more from any document. Financial data is validated against PE industry norms to catch extraction errors.

Chat with Deals

Ask natural language questions about any deal. The AI uses RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) to search across all documents for the deal and provide sourced answers. Example: "What's the revenue growth trend?" or "Summarize the management team."

Investment Memo Builder

Generate professional investment memos with AI-assisted sections: Executive Summary, Company Overview, Market Analysis, Financial Analysis, Investment Thesis, Risks, and Recommendation. Edit and customize each section.

Multi-Document Analysis

When a deal has 2+ documents, PE OS automatically cross-references them to detect conflicts (e.g., different revenue figures in CIM vs teaser), fill data gaps, and synthesize insights across all sources.

Virtual Data Room

Organize, share, and track deal documents securely

expand_more

Document Organization

Create folders and subfolders to organize deal documents. Supported types: CIM, Teaser, Financial Model, NDA, LOI, Due Diligence reports, and custom document types.

File Management

Upload, download, preview, and delete documents. Supported formats include PDF, Word, Excel, PowerPoint, and images. Maximum file size: 50MB per file.

Folder Insights

AI-generated insights for each folder summarize key points across all documents within. Get an instant overview of what's in each section of your data room.

group

Team & Permissions

Manage users, roles, and access control for your workspace

expand_more

Workspace Roles

Admin — full access: manage users, invite team, delete deals, view audit logs, export data. Member — standard access: create/edit deals, upload documents, use AI features. Viewer — read-only access to deals and documents.

Invitations

Admins can invite new team members via email. Invitations expire after 7 days and can be revoked. Invited users join the same workspace and firm with the assigned role.

Display Titles

Set custom display titles (Managing Director, Vice President, Associate, Analyst) independently of system roles. Titles are visible to teammates but don't affect permissions.

shield

Security & Compliance

Encryption, audit trails, and data protection for regulated PE firms

expand_more

Data Encryption

AES-256-GCM encryption at rest for sensitive deal fields. TLS 1.3 for all data in transit. Encryption keys are managed server-side and never exposed to clients.

Immutable Audit Trail

Every action is logged — deal creation, edits, document uploads, AI extractions, exports, logins. Audit logs are INSERT-ONLY (cannot be modified or deleted) for SEC/regulatory compliance.

Data Export

Export your deal data in CSV or JSON format at any time. Every export is audit-logged. Your data is yours — full portability guaranteed.
